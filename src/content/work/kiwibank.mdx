<Section label="Research" title="The gap was between reading and understanding">
  Banking websites are built around the assumption that customers already know what they are looking for. Research across 25 participants, interviewed one-on-one in six locations across New Zealand, told a different story. There was a consistent gap between customers reading information and actually understanding it.

  The research explored not just the website but how people relate to money more broadly. Key themes around confidence, context, and trust shaped a core design principle: support every key customer decision with information that is relevant and helpful, meeting people where they are.

<Section label="Framework" title="Four modes, one experience">
  Research identified four distinct modes customers arrive in, each requiring a different kind of support. These became the foundation for the content model and interaction patterns across the site.

  <CardGrid cols={2}>
    <Card title="Find" description="Customers who know what they want. Clear pathways get them there quickly." />
    <Card title="Explore" description="Customers with a goal but no clear path. The site understands their context and helps them orient." />
    <Card title="Learn" description="Customers building understanding before they can decide. Plain language and tools make concepts concrete." />
    <Card title="Decide" description="Customers ready to commit. Depth and transparency give them the confidence to act." />
  </CardGrid>
</Section>

<Section label="Design" title="Start with what you are trying to do">
  People do not think in product categories. They think in situations. The homepage reflected that with a natural language entry point: customers could describe what they were trying to achieve, and the site would surface relevant knowledge articles, tools, and products around that goal.

  It was a direct expression of the research, and the clearest demonstration of the framework in action.
</Section>

<ImageFull src="/images/kiwibank/kb-natural-language.png" alt="Natural language entry point on the Kiwibank homepage" />

<Section label="Design System" title="Adaptable without losing coherence">
  The design system was built to serve four very different customer modes without fragmenting the experience. Components were optimised for their specific role, whether that was an interaction, a decision point, or a piece of information. Adaptability and consistency were not in tension. They were designed together.
</Section>
